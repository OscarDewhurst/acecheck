How AceCheck Works

1. Player scans the QR code

No apps, no accounts required. Just scan and play.

2. Cameras automatically record the shot

A discreet green-side camera captures the strike and the ball's landing.

3. AI or staff verification

Early stage: verified manually. Later: AI determines landing distance.

4. Immediate results

Hole-in-one prizes, refunds, or new “points-based” challenge system.
