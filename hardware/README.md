# Hardware Documentation

## Cameras (PTZ External Camera)
- 4K PTZ camera
- 20x optical zoom
- Weatherproof (IP66+)
- POE or solar-powered
- 4G router connectivity

## Solar Setup
- 40W–60W panel
- 12V deep cycle battery
- Charge controller
- Small metal housing

## Mounting
- 3–4m pole near green
- Camera angle: focused on pin/green surface
- Anti-tamper screws
