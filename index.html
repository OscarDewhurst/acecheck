What Is AceCheck?

AceCheck is a hole-in-one challenge for golf courses. Golfers scan a QR code on a par 3 tee box, pay a small fee, and win prizes or refunds based on their shot. A camera overlooking the green records and verifies every attempt.

Why Golf Courses Love It

  • Additional revenue with zero cost to the club
  • Fast install, no course disruption
  • Golfers enjoy an exciting challenge
  • All verification handled by AceCheck
